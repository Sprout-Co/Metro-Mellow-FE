# Metromellow SEO & Copywriting Guide

## Overview
This guide ensures consistent, SEO-optimized copywriting across all pages of the Metromellow website. It provides guidelines for tone, structure, keywords, and best practices to maintain brand voice while maximizing search visibility.

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Principles
- **Professional yet approachable**: Confident without being corporate
- **Benefit-focused**: Always lead with what the customer gains
- **Location-specific**: Emphasize Lagos and Nigerian context
- **Trust-building**: Include social proof and guarantees
- **Action-oriented**: Clear, compelling CTAs

### Tone Guidelines
- ✅ **DO**: "Professional home services that give you back your time"
- ❌ **DON'T**: "We provide services for your home"
- ✅ **DO**: "Join 1,000+ satisfied Lagos families"
- ❌ **DON'T**: "Many customers trust us"

## SEO Strategy

### Primary Keywords
**Location + Service Combinations:**
- "home services Lagos"
- "professional cleaning Lagos"
- "laundry service Lagos"
- "meal delivery Lagos"
- "pest control Lagos"

**Long-tail Keywords:**
- "best home services in Lagos"
- "professional cleaning services Lagos"
- "fresh meal delivery Lagos"
- "same-day laundry service Lagos"
- "trusted home services Lagos"

### Keyword Density Guidelines
- **Primary keyword**: 2-3 times per page
- **Secondary keywords**: 1-2 times per page
- **Location keywords**: Include "Lagos" in every major section
- **Natural integration**: Never force keywords into awkward sentences

## Page Structure & Headlines

### H1 Guidelines
- Include primary keyword
- Maximum 60 characters
- Clear benefit + location
- **Examples:**
  - "Professional Home Services in Lagos"
  - "Best Cleaning Services in Lagos"
  - "Fresh Meal Delivery in Lagos"

### H2 Guidelines
- Support primary keyword
- Include secondary keywords
- Clear benefit statements
- **Examples:**
  - "Professional House Cleaning That Actually Works"
  - "Fresh, Nutritious Meals Delivered Daily"
  - "Complete Laundry Service - Pickup to Delivery"

### H3 Guidelines
- Service-specific benefits
- Include location when relevant
- **Examples:**
  - "Deep Cleaning Services"
  - "Traditional Nigerian Dishes"
  - "Eco-Friendly Pest Control"

## Content Guidelines by Page Type

### Home Page
**Hero Section:**
- Primary keyword in headline
- 3-4 key services mentioned
- Location prominently featured
- Strong, action-oriented CTA

**Service Sections:**
- Benefit-focused headlines
- Specific service descriptions
- Trust signals (ratings, guarantees)
- Location-specific benefits

### Service Pages
**Page Title:** "[Service] in Lagos | Professional [Service] | Metromellow"
**Meta Description:** "Professional [service] in Lagos. [Key benefits]. Same-day service available. Book online today!"

**Content Structure:**
1. Hero with service-specific headline
2. Benefits section with 3-5 key points
3. Process explanation (How it works)
4. Pricing/options
5. FAQ section
6. CTA section

### About Page
**Focus Keywords:**
- "home services company Lagos"
- "professional cleaning team Lagos"
- "trusted home services Nigeria"

**Content Elements:**
- Company story and mission
- Team credentials and experience
- Service area coverage
- Customer testimonials
- Trust badges and certifications

## Copywriting Templates

### Service Descriptions
**Template:** "[Service type] for [target audience] in [location]. [Key benefit 1], [Key benefit 2], and [Key benefit 3]. [Trust signal]."

**Example:** "Professional house cleaning for busy families in Lagos. Deep cleaning, regular maintenance, and move-in services. Vetted professionals with quality supplies and satisfaction guarantee."

### CTA Guidelines
**Primary CTAs:**
- "Get Started Today"
- "Book [Service] Now"
- "Start Your Free Trial"
- "Download Our App"

**Secondary CTAs:**
- "Learn More"
- "Browse Services"
- "View Pricing"
- "Contact Us"

### Trust Signals
**Include in every major section:**
- "1,000+ satisfied customers"
- "Vetted professionals"
- "Satisfaction guarantee"
- "Same-day service available"
- "Licensed professionals"
- "Eco-friendly options"

## Technical SEO Guidelines

### Meta Tags
**Title Tag Format:** "[Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Keyword] | Metromellow"
- Maximum 60 characters
- Include location
- Include brand name

**Meta Description Format:** "[Benefit statement]. [Key features]. [Location]. [CTA]."
- Maximum 160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Include location
- Include compelling CTA

### Schema Markup
**Required for all pages:**
- LocalBusiness schema
- Service schema
- FAQ schema
- Review schema (where applicable)

### Image Alt Text
**Format:** "[Service] in [Location] - [Description]"
- **Example:** "Professional cleaning service in Lagos - Deep cleaning residential home"

## Content Quality Standards

### Readability
- **Flesch Reading Ease:** 60-80 (Easy to read)
- **Sentence length:** Maximum 20 words
- **Paragraph length:** Maximum 3-4 sentences
- **Use bullet points** for lists and benefits

### Proofreading Checklist
- [ ] All headlines include primary keyword
- [ ] Location mentioned in every major section
- [ ] Trust signals included
- [ ] CTAs are action-oriented
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting
